About BCIIT

WELCOME TO BCIIT Banarsidas Chandiwala Institute of Information Technology (BCIIT) was established in 1999 to run Master of Computer Application (MCA) programme. The Institute is affiliated with Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University( GGSIPU), Delhi for awarding the degree and is approved by the All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE), Ministry of HRD, Government of India.The Institute is established under the aegis of Sri Banarsidas Chandiwala Sewa Smarak Trust Society(SBCSSTS), a charitable Society working in the field of health and education since 1952.

Director of BCIIT

DR. Ravish saggar

Dr Ravish Saggar has fourteen plus years of teaching and two years industry (Software Development) experience. He has done B.Tech. (Computer Technology) with first division from Nagpur University and M.Tech (IT) with first division from G.G.S.Indraspastha University, Delhi. He has taught Data Structures and VB 6 at graduate level and DBMS, Operating System, Data Structures, Linux at postgraduate level.
